E-commerce Sales Performance Dashboard
Project Overview
Yeh project Ecom Express ke sales data ka ek end-to-end data analysis hai. Is dashboard ka maqsad business ke key performance indicators (KPIs) ko track karna, revenue trends ko samajhna, aur business ke growth areas aur potential risks (jaise high cancellation rate) ko identify karna hai.

Key Insights (Summary)
Revenue Performance: MacBook Air aur OnePlus 9 platform ke top-performing products hain, jo revenue mein sabse bada contribution de rahe hain.

Category Analysis: 'Laptop' aur 'Mobile' categories platform ki revenue backbone hain, jabki 'Cable' aur 'Speaker' categories mein abhi kaafi scope for improvement hai.

Geographical Reach: Maharashtra aur Gujarat sales ke maamle mein leading states hain, jo overall revenue growth ko drive kar rahe hain.

Business Concern: Dashboard 29.72% ka ek significant Cancellation Rate dikhata hai, jiske kaaran $525.4M ka revenue loss ho raha hai. Yeh business operations ke liye ek major focus point hai.

Quarterly Trend: Revenue trend Q1 se Q3 tak stable raha hai, lekin Q4 mein ek sharp decline dekha gaya hai, jise address karne ki zaroorat hai.

Strategic Recommendations
Reduce Cancellation Rate: Cancellation ke reasons (e.g., delivery delays, product quality issues) ko investigate karke operational efficiency badhani hogi.

Focus on Low-Performing Categories: Cable aur Speaker segments mein targeted marketing ya festive discounts dekar sales ko badhaya ja sakta hai.

Regional Growth: Telangana aur Delhi jaise states mein marketing spend badhakar wahan ke market penetration ko improve kiya ja sakta hai.
